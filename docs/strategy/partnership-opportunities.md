## 7. Monetization Partnership Models

### 7.1 Revenue Share Model

**How it works:**

```
Partner (e.g., YC) features OpenHR
        |
        v
User joins from partner link
        |
        v
User converts to paid tier
        |
        v
OpenHR pays partner 20-30% of subscription revenue
```

**Example:**
- Partner brings 1000 users
- 10% convert to paid ($99/year) = 100 users
- Revenue: $9,900
- Partner gets: $1,980-2,970
- OpenHR keeps: $6,930

### 7.2 Fixed Fee Model

**How it works:**

```
Partner brings users --> OpenHR pays monthly fee ($5K-50K) --> Exclusive integration
```

**Best for:**
- Large platforms (AngelList, Crunchbase)
- Enterprise-level access

### 7.3 Equity Model (Future)

**For strategic partners only:**

```
Partner invests in OpenHR --> Gets equity stake --> Revenue share
```

**Example:** VCs invest $100K for 1% equity + 25% revenue share (while equity owned)

## 10. Partnership Agreement Template

### Key Sections

```markdown
# OpenHR Partnership Agreement

## 1. Definitions
- **Partner:** [Organization name]
- **Term:** [1 year, renewable]
- **Effective Date:** [Date]

## 2. Grant of Rights
**OpenHR grants Partner the right to:**
- Feature OpenHR co-founder matching in [Partner platform/communications]
- Provide [free/premium] access to Partner users
- White-label (optional) with Partner branding

## 3. Partner Obligations
**Partner agrees to:**
- Feature OpenHR to [N] users/month
- Provide co-marketing support (blog, email, announcements)
- Share [anonymized] analytics on user engagement
- Maintain link/integration in [location]

## 4. Financial Terms
**Revenue share:** [X%] of premium subscriptions from Partner users
**Minimum guarantee:** None (performance-based)
**Payment terms:** Monthly, net 30

## 5. Data & Privacy
- OpenHR owns user data
- Partner can access [anonymized analytics only]
- GDPR compliant
- No third-party sharing

## 6. Term & Termination
- Initial term: 1 year
- Auto-renews unless 60 days notice
- Either party can terminate with 90 days notice
- Wind-down period: 30 days

## 7. Confidentiality
- Both parties keep partnership confidential until public announcement
- Can mention partnership in marketing after mutual agreement

## 8. IP & Branding
- OpenHR owns all platform IP
- Partner owns their branding
- Neither party modifies other's branding without consent
```
